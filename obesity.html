What is Obesity?

Obesity is a medical condition where excess body fat accumulates, increasing health risks.

Causes of Obesity

Common causes include poor diet, lack of physical activity, genetics, and medical conditions.

Symptoms

Symptoms include excessive body fat, difficulty in physical activity, and increased BMI.

Health Risks of Obesity

Obesity increases the risk of heart disease, diabetes, high blood pressure, and joint problems.

Treatment Options

Treatment includes lifestyle changes, diet plans, exercise, medication, and surgery in severe cases.

Prevention Strategies

Healthy eating, regular exercise, and lifestyle changes can prevent obesity.
