Vitamins & Their Benefits

What Are Vitamins?

Vitamins are essential nutrients required for various bodily functions, including metabolism, immunity, and cell repair. They help maintain overall health and well-being.

Vitamin A

Benefits: Supports vision, immune function, and skin health.

Sources: Carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach,fish,meat and dairy products.

Vitamin B

Benefits: Helps in energy production, brain function, and red blood cell formation.

Sources: Whole grains, meat, eggs, dairy, and leafy greens.

Vitamin C

Benefits: Boosts immunity, promotes healthy skin, and aids iron absorption.

Sources: Citrus fruits, strawberries, bell peppers, and broccoli.

Vitamin D

Benefits: Supports bone health, immune function, and mood regulation.

Sources: Sunlight, fatty fish, fortified dairy, and egg yolks.

Vitamin E

Benefits: Acts as an antioxidant, supports skin and eye health.

Sources: Nuts, seeds, spinach, and vegetable oils.

Vitamin K

Benefits: Essential for blood clotting and bone health.

Sources: Leafy greens, broccoli, and dairy products.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the essential vitamins for daily health?

Essential vitamins include Vitamin A, B-complex, C, D, E, and K, each playing a role in bodily functions.

Can I get all vitamins from food?

A balanced diet can provide most vitamins, but some people may need supplements for specific deficiencies.

How does Vitamin D help the body?

Vitamin D helps with calcium absorption, bone health, immune support, and mood regulation.

What is the best source of Vitamin C?

Citrus fruits, strawberries, bell peppers, and broccoli are excellent sources of Vitamin C.

Why is Vitamin K important?

Vitamin K is crucial for blood clotting and bone metabolism.
