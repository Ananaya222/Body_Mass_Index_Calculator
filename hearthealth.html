Heart Health

Heart health is crucial for overall well-being, as the heart plays a vital role in pumping blood and supplying oxygen and nutrients to the body. Maintaining a healthy heart involves a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while limiting saturated fats, processed foods, and excessive salt. Regular physical activity, such as walking, jogging, or strength training, helps improve cardiovascular fitness and reduce the risk of heart disease.Managing stress, avoiding smoking, limiting alcohol intake, and maintaining a healthy weight are also essential for heart health. Additionally, regular check-ups to monitor blood pressure, cholesterol levels, and blood sugar can help detect potential issues early and prevent serious heart conditions like hypertension, heart attacks, and strokes. Prioritizing a heart-healthy lifestyle significantly enhances longevity and quality of life.

Coronary Artery Disease

Coronary artery disease (CAD) is a condition where the blood vessels that supply the heart muscle become narrowed or blocked.

Causes:

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes
  • Obesity

Prevention:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Avoid smoking
  • Control blood pressure and cholesterol levels

Heart Attack

A heart attack occurs when blood flow to the heart is blocked, often due to a blood clot.

Causes:

  • Blocked arteries
  • Smoking
  • High cholesterol
  • Stress
  • Diabetes

Prevention:

  • Healthy diet
  • Regular exercise
  • Avoid smoking
  • Manage stress
  • Control diabetes and cholesterol levels

Arrhythmia

Arrhythmia is an irregular heartbeat that occurs when the electrical signals to the heart are not functioning properly.

Causes:

  • High blood pressure
  • Heart disease
  • Electrolyte imbalance
  • Stress
  • Smoking

Prevention:

  • Reduce stress
  • Limit caffeine and alcohol
  • Exercise regularly
  • Monitor heart rate
  • Avoid smoking
